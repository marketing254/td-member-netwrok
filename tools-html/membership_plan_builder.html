How this tool works

What it does

It prices an in-house membership plan for uninsured patients: what the member pays, what care they receive, whether they clearly win, and what the plan means in predictable yearly revenue for the practice.

What to gather before you start

  • Monthly membership fee: your planned price; keep it simple to say at the front desk
  • Value of included care: price 2 cleanings, 1 exam, and x-rays from YOUR own fee schedule
  • Member discount on other treatment: your planned percentage, commonly 10-15%
  • Expected members in year one: run a PMS report of active patients WITHOUT insurance; they are your starting pool
  • Extra treatment per member (optional): your average yearly treatment revenue per uninsured patient

The math, in plain English

Plan revenue = fee x 12 x members. The member's saving = included care value minus what they pay per year; if that number is not clearly positive, the tool warns you to fix the design. Treatment revenue applies your member discount to the extra treatment you expect.

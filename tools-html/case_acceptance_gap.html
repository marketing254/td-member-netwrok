How this tool works

What it does

It converts the gap between your current case acceptance rate and your target into dollars per year, on treatment you have already diagnosed and presented.

What to gather before you start

  • Treatment presented per month: most PMS systems report treatment planned or presented per period; use a typical month
  • Current acceptance rate: treatment accepted divided by treatment presented, same report
  • Target acceptance rate: the benchmark in the kit is 80% or better

The math, in plain English

The gap = presented treatment x 12 months x the difference between target and current acceptance. Each 1 point of acceptance is worth 1% of your yearly presented treatment. No new patients or chair time are assumed; it is the same dentistry with more yeses.

1. What you're joining

The Dental Member Network is a membership for dental practices. As a founding expert, your knowledge is featured to our members. This agreement covers how that works, what it costs, and how either of us can end it.

2. Term and cancellation

  • Month-to-month, with no long-term contract.
  • You can cancel anytime with 30 days' written notice. So can we.
  • We can end it immediately if you break these terms.
  • If you cancel and re-join later, any founding rate you had no longer applies.

3. What it costs

Your expert listing (your content and profile): free, for as long as your membership stays continuously active, our founding-expert offer. If you cancel and re-join, this no longer applies.

4. Using your content and name

  • You keep ownership of everything you create and share.
  • While this agreement is active, you give us permission to repurpose it into member resources and to use your name, photo, and company to do that and to market the network.
  • If you leave, we take down your profile, your listing, and any resources that feature only you, within 30 days. Content where you appear alongside others (panels, group sessions, podcasts) remains in the network. (Copies members already downloaded can't be recalled.)

5. Conduct and honesty

  • Be truthful in what you claim and what you offer members.
  • You may promote your own services to members who reach out. Our “no upsells” promise is about DMN never pushing members, not about limiting you.
  • Use the Thriving Dentist and DMN brands only as we agree.

6. The basics

  • You're an independent expert, not our employee or agent.
  • DMN connects members with experts and partners; we're not responsible for the advice you give or the products you sell, and you'll cover us for claims arising from your content or offerings.
  • Keep member information confidential and don't misuse it.

7. The fine print, kept short

  • All prices are in US dollars; applicable taxes are added on top.
  • If a payment fails, we'll retry and pause your listing until it's resolved. No pro-rated refunds for partial months.
  • We don't guarantee results, leads, or revenue, and the platform is provided as-is. We are not responsible for losses you may incur from this partnership. Our total liability to you is capped at the fees you've paid us in the previous 12 months, and neither of us is liable to the other for indirect or consequential losses.
  • In the resources we build from your content, the substance stays yours; our branding, design, and templates stay ours.
  • We can update these terms at any time.
  • Written notice means email, using the addresses on this agreement.
  • This agreement is governed by the laws of the Province of Ontario, Canada. Any dispute will be resolved through confidential binding arbitration in Ontario rather than in court.
  • This is our entire agreement. If one part can't be enforced, the rest still applies.

Schedule A: Expert

  • We build you a done-for-you resource library from your content, plus a featured profile in the member portal.
  • Members find you by category and contact you through your profile. We route member questions to you, but don't guarantee a number of leads.
  • You may list your own paid courses and keep 70% (we keep 30% to run the platform, payments, and promotion).
  • We don't offer exclusivity for our experts; members choose who they want to work with.

Accepting this agreement

No signature needed. This works like any online subscription.

  • You accept this agreement by ticking “I agree” at sign-up and entering your payment details.
  • Your acceptance is recorded electronically (who accepted, which version, and when), and a copy of this agreement is emailed to you. You can also download it anytime.
  • The details you provide at sign-up (your name, company, role, and member offer) form part of this agreement.

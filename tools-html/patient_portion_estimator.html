How this tool works

What it does

It gives the front desk a ten-second estimate of what insurance is likely to pay and what the patient owes, with a printable quote slip that carries the estimate-only disclaimer.

What to gather before you start

  • Treatment fee: the patient's treatment plan or your fee schedule
  • Coverage percentage: the breakdown of benefits from your eligibility verification; the common pattern is 100% preventive / 80% basic / 50% major
  • Deductible remaining: the carrier portal or eligibility call
  • Annual maximum remaining: same place; it shrinks with every claim in the benefit year

The math, in plain English

The remaining deductible comes off the fee first. The coverage percentage applies to what is left, and the result is capped at the remaining annual maximum (the tool warns you when the cap bites). The patient pays the rest. Always verify benefits: this is an estimate, never a guarantee.

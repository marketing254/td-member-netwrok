How this tool works

What it does

It computes the true monthly payment, total repayment, and total interest on a practice loan, plus the payment as a share of collections if you provide them.

What to gather before you start

  • Loan amount, rate, and term: the lender's term sheet or quote
  • Practice monthly collections (optional): your PMS collections report, to see the debt-service share

The math, in plain English

The payment uses the standard fixed-payment amortization formula every bank uses. Total repayment = payment x number of months; interest = total minus the amount borrowed. Run the rate half a percent up and down before negotiating, so you know what the difference is worth.

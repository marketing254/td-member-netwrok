How this tool works

What it does

It measures your overhead percentage against the under-60% benchmark for a healthy general practice, and shows what each point of overhead costs and what reaching the benchmark would free up.

What to gather before you start

  • Monthly collections: your PMS collections report for a typical month
  • Monthly overhead: your P&L from your bookkeeper or accounting software: all practice expenses EXCLUDING your own compensation

The math, in plain English

Overhead percentage = expenses divided by collections. Each 1% of overhead equals 1% of your yearly collections. The freed-up number is the yearly difference between your current expenses and what they would be at exactly 60%.

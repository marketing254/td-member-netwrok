How this tool works

What it does

It calculates what a new patient is truly worth over the years they stay, what you currently pay to attract one, and the lifetime return on every marketing dollar.

What to gather before you start

  • Average revenue per active patient per year: yearly production divided by your active patient count
  • Years an average patient stays: estimate from your own patient base; how long do typical patients remain active?
  • Monthly marketing spend: your bookkeeping; include ads, SEO, and directories
  • New patients per month from marketing: your referral-source tracking; the 'how did you hear about us' answer on the new-patient call

The math, in plain English

Lifetime value = yearly revenue per patient x years retained. Acquisition cost = monthly spend divided by monthly new patients. The return multiple = lifetime value divided by acquisition cost. Referrals are deliberately left out, so the real number is better than the one shown.

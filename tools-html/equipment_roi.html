How this tool works

What it does

It tells you how many months until a piece of equipment pays for itself, and the five-year return, using your own conservative numbers instead of the sales brochure's.

What to gather before you start

  • Equipment cost, all-in: the quote including installation and training
  • Extra revenue per month: procedures per month you would realistically add or keep in-house, times the fee you actually bill; be conservative
  • Extra running cost per month: service contract, per-use supplies, and financing interest if any

The math, in plain English

Net gain = extra revenue minus extra running cost. Payback = the all-in cost divided by the net gain. The five-year return = 60 months of net gain minus the purchase. Compare the payback to the machine's realistic service life before deciding.

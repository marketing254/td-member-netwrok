How this tool works

What it does

It values the patients sleeping in your database and shows what winning back even a small share of them is worth per year.

What to gather before you start

  • Inactive patients: a PMS report of patients not seen in 18+ months; exclude patients who moved away or passed
  • Average revenue per active patient per year: yearly production divided by active patient count
  • Win-back share: your own conservative assumption; test small before counting on it

The math, in plain English

Value asleep = inactive patients x yearly value per patient. Recovered revenue = that value x your win-back share. Each 1% you win back equals 1% of the sleeping value. These are patients you already paid to acquire once.

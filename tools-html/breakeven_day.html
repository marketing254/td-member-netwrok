How this tool works

What it does

It finds the time of day your practice has covered its own costs, so everything produced after that hour is yours. One memorable number for the whole team.

What to gather before you start

  • Monthly practice overhead: your P&L: everything it costs to open the doors, excluding your own pay
  • Working days per month: your calendar
  • Average production per day: monthly production divided by working days
  • Your working day: start time and length in hours

The math, in plain English

Daily break-even = overhead divided by working days. The share of the day working for the bills = daily break-even divided by daily production. The clock time maps that share onto your actual working hours.
